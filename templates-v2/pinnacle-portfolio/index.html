Case study

How Northline Foundry became impossible to confuse.

The team needed a premium identity that could flex across investor decks, venture launches, research storytelling, and recruiting without fragmenting into separate sub-brands.

Problem

Northline had strong technical credibility but no clear market signal. Each new company launch felt visually unrelated, and the parent brand struggled to communicate velocity without looking generic.

Approach

We built a modular identity platform: a precise wordmark, expandable grid language, venture color rules, a presentation system, and a gallery of launch-ready motion behaviors.

Result

The new system gave every team a shared creative operating model. Launches became faster, recruiting felt more polished, and the parent brand finally carried a premium point of view.
