# Spark Creator Economy Design Book

## Positioning

Spark Creator Economy is a premium creator monetization dashboard for teams running audience growth, scheduled content, sponsorships, and revenue operations from one interface.

## Visual System

- Typography: Manrope for headings and brand moments, Inter for navigation, metrics, labels, tables, and controls.
- Background: Cloud `#F6F8FC` keeps the workspace quiet and operational.
- Text and dark panels: Ink `#19212B` anchors contrast and creates executive dashboard moments.
- Primary action: Indigo `#4F46E5` is used for navigation emphasis, action buttons, and core progress bars.
- Live signals: Cyan `#06B6D4` marks active audience movement, chart gradients, and live states.
- Healthy states: Lime `#A3E635` communicates ready, approved, and positive operating health.
- Alert states: Coral `#FB7185` flags urgent sponsorship terms, notifications, and editorial risk.

## Layout

The page opens as a dense creator command center rather than a marketing site. A sticky top navigation leads into a hero operations panel, KPI strip, content calendar, analytics, revenue, top content, sponsorship inbox, and credit footer.

## Components

- Top navigation with brand, primary sections, notification badge, avatar, and a keyboard-ready mobile drawer.
- KPI cards for followers, monthly views, engagement rate, and monthly revenue.
- Weekly calendar grid with platform markers, schedule times, post titles, and status badges.
- CSS-only donut chart for platform distribution with a 12-month growth bar chart.
- Revenue source tracker with CSS bars and a dark cash-flow summary panel.
- Top content table for performance review and sponsor attribution.
- Sponsorship cards with brand initials, deal budgets, deadlines, and negotiation status.

## Accessibility

Semantic landmarks, labelled navigation regions, aria state updates for the mobile drawer, visible focus outlines, Escape close behavior, basic focus containment, and reduced motion support are included in the standalone file.

## Responsive Behavior

Desktop uses a multi-column workspace and wide calendar board. Tablet collapses analytics and revenue grids. Mobile keeps the dashboard usable through a hamburger drawer, single-column KPI cards, scrollable calendar table, compact charts, and stacked sponsorship cards.
