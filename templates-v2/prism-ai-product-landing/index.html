FAQ

Common questions

How does the human override work?
When the AI's confidence is below your threshold, the decision is queued for human review. The reviewer can approve, modify, or reject. Every override feeds back into the model.
What happens if the AI makes a wrong decision?
Every decision is logged with a rollback option. You can reverse any action within the audit trail. The system learns from corrections to reduce future errors.
Is my data used to train your models?
No. Your data is never used to train shared models. Enterprise customers can opt for dedicated model instances isolated from other tenants.
How fast is the inference?
Average latency is 120ms for standard models and 45ms for optimized models. We guarantee 99.9% uptime SLA on Pro and Enterprise plans.
