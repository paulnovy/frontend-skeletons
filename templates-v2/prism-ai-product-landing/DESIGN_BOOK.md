# Prism AI Product Landing — Design Book

## Concept
AI product landing page that converts visitors into trial users. Hero with product demo, feature grid, social proof strip, use-case tabs, pricing, FAQ, and CTA section.

## Palette
- Cloud: #F6F8FC
- Ink: #19212B
- Indigo: #4F46E5
- Cyan: #06B6D4
- Lime: #A3E635
- Coral: #FB7185

## Typography
- Headings: Manrope
- UI: Inter

## Layout
- Hero with animated product preview and dual CTA
- Social proof strip with company logos and metrics
- Feature grid with icon cards
- Use-case tabs with contextual demos
- Pricing tiers with recommended plan highlighted
- FAQ accordion
- Final CTA section with urgency messaging

## Responsive
- Mobile: stacked hero, feature cards, social proof carousel, pricing comparison scroll
- Tablet: 2-column feature grid, pricing cards side-by-side
- Desktop: full-width hero, 3-column features, pricing table

## Accessibility
- Semantic landmarks
- Keyboard-navigable tabs, accordion, hamburger
- Reduced-motion support
- Focus-visible states
