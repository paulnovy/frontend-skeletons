# Terra Sustainability Hub Design Book

## Concept
A premium corporate sustainability hub for ESG reporting, initiative tracking, carbon performance, and board-ready reporting.

## Visual System
- Typography: Manrope for headings and Inter for interface text via Google Fonts.
- Palette: Cloud page surfaces, ink text and dark command panels, indigo primary actions, cyan live signals, lime healthy states, and coral risk states.
- Layout: Dense executive dashboard sections with KPI strips, scorecards, progress meters, and CSS-only data visualization.

## Interaction
- Sticky desktop navigation with anchor links.
- Mobile hamburger opens a keyboard-accessible slide-in drawer.
- Focus-visible styles and reduced-motion support are included.

## Sections
Top navigation, KPI strip, ESG scorecards, carbon footprint tracker, green initiatives, sustainability goals, downloadable reports, and footer credit.
