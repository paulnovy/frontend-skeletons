# Atlas Food Delivery Design Book

## Identity
Atlas Food Delivery is a polished operations interface for restaurant marketplace teams, kitchen managers, dispatchers, and growth leads. The experience balances marketplace browsing with live logistics so a single screen can move from discovery to delivery health.

## Typography
- Headings use Manrope for a confident operational voice.
- Interface text uses Inter for dense, readable controls and metrics.

## Palette
- Cloud `#F6F8FC` for the application canvas.
- Ink `#19212B` for primary text and high-contrast surfaces.
- Indigo `#4F46E5` for primary action and active state.
- Cyan `#06B6D4` for live logistics and chart energy.
- Lime `#A3E635` for healthy status and featured emphasis.
- Coral `#FB7185` for cart count and warm alerts.

## Layout Notes
The page is a standalone single-file skeleton with sticky navigation, restaurant cards, active order timelines, a menu builder, driver dispatch cards, analytics metrics, and a mobile hamburger drawer. Cards use restrained 8px radii to keep the product feeling like an operational platform rather than a campaign page.
