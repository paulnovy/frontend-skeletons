# Terra Renewable Energy

A standalone renewable energy dashboard skeleton for solar and wind monitoring, energy production, carbon offset tracking, and grid management.

## Files
- `index.html` — single-file HTML with inline CSS and JavaScript.
- `DESIGN_BOOK.md` — visual system, typography, palette, and layout notes.
- `README.md` — project summary.

## Requirements Covered
- Top navigation with brand, section links, search, live indicator, and mobile drawer.
- KPI strip for generation, capacity, carbon offset, and grid health.
- CSS stacked production chart by source across seven days.
- Site monitoring cards with type, capacity, output, and efficiency.
- Grid management gauge, demand and supply metrics, and battery levels.
- Carbon tracking with offsets, projects, and certifications.
- Energy forecast panels.
- Footer credit: Terra Renewable Energy — designed by Studio.

Open `index.html` in a browser to view the dashboard.
