Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my certification progress?

Each certification shows a progress bar based on completed modules. Your overall dashboard aggregates all active certifications into a unified view.

Can I schedule exam reminders?

Yes, use the Study Planner to set up recurring study blocks and exam date reminders. Notifications are sent via email and in-app alerts.

Are practice exam scores tracked?

All exam simulator attempts are logged with scores, time spent, and per-category breakdowns to identify weak areas.

How do I verify a certificate?

Each certificate has a unique verification link. Click Verify to open the public verification page with credential details.
