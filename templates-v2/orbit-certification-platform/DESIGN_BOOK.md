# Orbit Certification Platform Design Book

## Concept

Orbit Certification Platform is a professional credentialing interface for learners and training teams. The design emphasizes a calm, operational workspace with clear progress, exam readiness, and certificate management.

## Typography

- Headings use Manrope for confident, compact hierarchy.
- Interface text uses Inter for readable controls, metrics, and dense dashboard content.

## Palette

- Cloud `#F6F8FC` sets the workspace background.
- Ink `#19212B` anchors text, dark panels, and brand elements.
- Indigo `#4F46E5` is the primary action and progress color.
- Cyan `#06B6D4` supports exam and analytics accents.
- Lime `#A3E635` signals completion and readiness.
- Coral `#FB7185` highlights timers, urgency, and review states.

## Layout

The page is a single standalone HTML skeleton with a sticky top navigation, responsive hamburger drawer, KPI strip, certification catalog, study planner, exam simulator, certificate gallery, progress dashboard, and footer credit.

## Components

- Certification cards include level badges, duration metadata, lesson counts, exam counts, and progress bars.
- Study planner rows use a three-column schedule on desktop and a stacked layout on mobile.
- Exam simulator includes answer selection, a timer, progress bar, and question navigation markers.
- Certificates use a credential seal, issue and expiry dates, and download actions.
- Progress dashboard combines domain bars, readiness mix, and recent activity.

## Responsiveness

Desktop uses multi-column grids for scanning. Tablet collapses heavy areas into two columns. Mobile switches to a drawer navigation and single-column content for focused review.
