# Aurora Product Showcase Design Book

## Concept
Aurora Product Showcase is a premium single-product marketing skeleton for a polished hardware launch page. It balances emotional product storytelling with practical buyer details: deep feature sections, technical specs, gallery moments, verified reviews, and a compact purchase flow.

## Visual System
- Typography: Manrope for headings and Inter for interface text, loaded from Google Fonts.
- Page background: Cloud `#F6F8FC`.
- Primary text and dark panels: Ink `#19212B`.
- Primary action: Indigo `#4F46E5`.
- Live indicators and focus accents: Cyan `#06B6D4`.
- Healthy status: Lime `#A3E635`.
- Critical states and warmer contrast: Coral `#FB7185`.

Only the required colors and transparent tints of those colors are used.

## Layout
The page is organized as a conversion-focused sequence: sticky navigation, product hero, alternating feature deep-dives, dark specification grid, gallery, customer proof, and purchase form. Cards use restrained radius values and strong spacing so the page feels premium without becoming decorative.

## Interaction
The mobile navigation uses a keyboard-accessible slide-in drawer. The purchase card includes finish choices, warranty options, quantity controls, and a live total. Gallery and feature visuals use subtle hover scaling, with reduced motion support for users who prefer less animation.

## Accessibility
Semantic landmarks, skip link, visible focus states, aria labels, accessible form controls, keyboard drawer closing, and `prefers-reduced-motion` are included in the skeleton.
