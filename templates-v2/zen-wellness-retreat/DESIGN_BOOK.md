# Zen Wellness Retreat Design Book

## Concept

Zen Wellness Retreat is a premium wellness business skeleton for retreat discovery, practitioner trust-building, guided meditation sampling, and direct booking. The interface feels calm, operationally clear, and polished enough for a boutique retreat brand.

## Visual System

- Typography: Manrope for display headings and Inter for navigation, controls, forms, and body copy.
- Palette: Cloud `#F6F8FC`, ink `#19212B`, indigo `#4F46E5`, cyan `#06B6D4`, lime `#A3E635`, and coral `#FB7185`.
- Layout: airy sections, 8px cards, crisp borders, strong contrast, and a dark meditation band for focus.
- Motion: subtle drawer and button motion with `prefers-reduced-motion` support.

## Sections

- Top navigation with desktop links, Book Now CTA, and mobile hamburger drawer.
- Hero with location badge, retreat search, date field, program selector, and gradient-backed availability card.
- Program exploration cards for Yoga, Meditation, Detox, and Sound Healing.
- Practitioner cards with initials, specialties, ratings, bio snippets, and availability badges.
- Meditation guide list with play buttons, durations, difficulty, and guide names.
- Three-step booking form with visible progress indicator.
- Testimonials with guest names, attended retreat, and ratings.
- Footer credit: Zen Wellness Retreat — designed by Stillpoint Studio.

## Accessibility

The page uses semantic landmarks, descriptive labels, visible focus states, a skip link, keyboard-operable drawer controls, Escape-to-close behavior, focus trapping inside the drawer, and reduced motion handling.
