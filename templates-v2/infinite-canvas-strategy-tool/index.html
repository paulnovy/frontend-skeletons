Board-ready strategy brief

The same strategy is always available as a readable document for stakeholders who need a linear review path.

1. Vision

StrataFlow will help logistics teams detect inventory risk before delay, giving mid-market operators predictive replenishment without an enterprise data program.

2. Values

Forecast transparency is the trust layer. Every recommendation should show lead time, demand movement, supplier confidence, and the operational consequence of waiting.

3. Positioning

The product should be sold as an inventory control tower connected to existing ERP workflows, not as a replacement planning spreadsheet.

4. Pricing

The commercial model combines a base platform license, SKU coverage bands, and optional supplier-risk intelligence for teams with volatile supply chains.

5. Roadmap

The next two quarters prioritize forecast explanations, exception queues, and approval trails before automated purchase order creation.
