Gardening Tips

Seasonal

💧 Watering in Summer

Water deeply and less frequently to encourage deep root growth. Early morning watering reduces evaporation and helps prevent fungal diseases.

Watering

🐛 Companion Planting

Plant marigolds near tomatoes to deter whiteflies. Basil planted alongside peppers improves flavor and repels aphids naturally.

Pest Control

🌾 Succession Planting

Sow lettuce and beans every 2–3 weeks for continuous harvest. This extends your growing season without overwhelming your kitchen.

Planning

🧪 Soil Testing

Test soil pH and nutrients every spring. Most vegetables thrive in pH 6.0–7.0. Amend with compost or sulfur as needed.
