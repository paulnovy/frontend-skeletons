# Zen Meditation App Design Book

## Identity
Zen Meditation App is a premium wellness interface for guided meditation, breathing practice, progress review, achievement tracking, and ambient sound mixing.

## Typography
- Headings use Manrope from Google Fonts.
- Interface text uses Inter from Google Fonts.

## Color System
- Cloud `#F6F8FC` for the page background and calm surfaces.
- Ink `#19212B` for text and deep contrast.
- Indigo `#4F46E5` for primary action and guidance states.
- Cyan `#06B6D4` for live indicators and secondary motion.
- Lime `#A3E635` for healthy streak and achievement signals.
- Coral `#FB7185` for stress and alert category accents.

## Layout
The skeleton uses a sticky top navigation, a mobile slide-in drawer, a first-screen daily practice hero, then dense product sections for sessions, breathing, progress, soundscapes, and badges. Cards use 8px radii and restrained surfaces to keep the app calm and operational.

## Accessibility
The page includes semantic landmarks, a skip link, keyboard-accessible drawer controls, focus-visible styles, explicit labels for controls, and reduced-motion handling.
