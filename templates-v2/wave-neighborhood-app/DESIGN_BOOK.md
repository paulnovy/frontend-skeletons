# Wave Neighborhood App Design Book

## Concept
Wave Neighborhood App is a premium hyperlocal community interface for a neighborhood social feed, neighbor discovery, local business browsing, event coordination, and safety alerts.

## Visual System
- Typography: Manrope for headings, Inter for interface copy, loaded from Google Fonts.
- Colors: Cloud `#F6F8FC`, ink `#19212B`, indigo `#4F46E5`, cyan `#06B6D4`, lime `#A3E635`, coral `#FB7185`, plus opacity-based tints derived from those values.
- Shape: tight 8px radius cards and controls for a civic product feel.
- Motion: subtle drawer and hover transitions with reduced-motion support.

## Structure
The single-file skeleton includes top navigation, mobile drawer, community feed, neighborhood stats, neighbor directory, local business directory, event board, safety alerts, and footer credit.

## Accessibility
Semantic landmarks, skip link, aria labels, focus-visible styling, keyboard Escape handling, and reduced-motion media query are included.
