# Arch Agency Site Design Book

## Positioning
Arch Agency Site is a premium digital agency skeleton for service showcase pages, team credibility, case study storytelling, and direct project inquiry.

## Visual System
- Typography: Manrope for headings and brand moments, Inter for navigation, forms, and body content.
- Palette: Cloud `#F6F8FC`, ink `#19212B`, indigo `#4F46E5`, cyan `#06B6D4`, lime `#A3E635`, and coral `#FB7185` with transparent tints.
- Shape: crisp 8px cards for repeatable content, larger framing only for the hero dashboard surface.

## Sections
- Top navigation with desktop links, CTA, and keyboard-accessible mobile drawer.
- Hero with strategic headline, dual CTAs, client logo strip, and live dashboard composition.
- Services, case studies, team profiles, process, contact form, and footer credit.

## Accessibility
Semantic landmarks, visible focus states, skip link, reduced motion handling, labeled inputs, and keyboard drawer controls are included.
