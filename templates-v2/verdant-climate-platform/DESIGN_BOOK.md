# Verdant Climate Platform - Design Book

## Project Name
Verdant Climate Platform

## Product Type
Climate tech SaaS homepage for emissions management, supplier evidence, compliance readiness, and ROI planning.

## Intended Audience
Sustainability leaders, CFOs, procurement teams, operations teams, auditors, and B2B SaaS buyers evaluating climate data platforms.

## User Problem
Climate data often stays trapped in reporting spreadsheets and vague green claims. Business teams need to understand what changed, what evidence supports it, and which action should happen next.

## UX Concept
The page translates climate data into business actions through an emissions dashboard preview, regulatory timeline, supplier evidence cards, audit trail, and ROI calculator.

## Layout Structure
Sticky navigation, trust-heavy hero, emissions dashboard preview, regulatory deadline cards, supplier data cards, audit trail section, ROI calculator CTA, mobile emissions summary, and footer.

## Typography System
Space Grotesk is used for decisive B2B headlines. Inter handles product UI and explanatory copy. IBM Plex Mono is used for emissions metrics, timestamps, and numeric evidence.

## Color System
Deep green `#073B2C`, mist `#E8F5E9`, leaf `#4ADE80`, cyan `#67E8F9`, earth `#A16207`, and ink `#10231D`.

## Component System
Core components include drawer navigation, emissions metric cards, bar chart preview, deadline cards, supplier confidence cards, audit log rows, ROI panel, and sticky mobile summary.

## Interaction Rules
Primary CTAs point to evidence and ROI rather than vague sustainability promises. Drawer state updates `aria-expanded` and `aria-hidden`.

## Responsive Behavior
Desktop balances story and dashboard preview. Tablet stacks operational sections. Mobile prioritizes emissions summary, compliance deadlines, supplier actions, and sticky summary.

## Accessibility Notes
Semantic landmarks, skip link, labeled navigation, visible focus states, readable contrast, descriptive actions, accessible drawer state, and reduced-motion fallback are included.

## Performance Notes
Standalone HTML with inline CSS, minimal JavaScript, CSS-only charts, stable layout dimensions, and no heavy dependencies.

## Commercial Use Cases
Climate SaaS homepage, ESG reporting product, supplier evidence portal, CSRD readiness campaign, assurance workflow prototype, and CFO-facing ROI landing page.
