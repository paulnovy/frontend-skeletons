Narrative Splitter

The interface treats contradiction as first-class data.

Every story separates what happened, who says it happened, what remains unverified, and what was corrected. Glitch effects are used only to reveal conflicting narratives, never to obscure confidence labels.

  • ConfirmedEvents supported by documents, named institutions, or multiple independent records.
