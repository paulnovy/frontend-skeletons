# InkLine — Editorial Media

A premium editorial media skeleton for digital magazines, long-form journalism, and multimedia storytelling.

## Overview

InkLine is a content-first editorial layout designed for publications that prioritize reading experience and content discovery. It features an asymmetric article grid, topic-based content rails, audio-article hybrids, and a newsletter conversion block.

## Features

- **Lead story hero** — Full-width editorial split with image and headline
- **Asymmetric article grid** — Mixed-height cards for visual rhythm
- **Topic rails** — Horizontal content sections organized by category
- **Audio-article hybrids** — Listen-and-read content cards
- **Newsletter block** — Dark-background email capture with strong CTA
- **Reading time indicators** — Visible commitment estimates on every article
- **Responsive** — Adapts from desktop (3-col) to mobile (single-col)

## Typography

- **Headlines:** Newsreader (Google Fonts) — editorial serif
- **Body/UI:** Inter (Google Fonts) — clean sans-serif

Open `index.html` in any modern browser. No build step or dependencies required.

## Customization

Replace SVG placeholder images with real photography. Wire up the newsletter form to your email provider. Connect audio players to your CMS. Swap topic categories to match your editorial sections.
