What happens after you book

A guided process with no mystery middle.

Mobile simplifies this into three clear steps so the consult CTA feels low-risk.

Week 1

Listen and measure

We audit the room, document constraints, and agree the decisions that matter most.

Week 2

Design and source

You receive layouts, material direction, and a realistic sourcing list with alternates.

Week 3

Install and tune

We coordinate trades, place key pieces, and adjust styling so the room works daily.
